Frequently Asked Questions







How many flavors do you have?

We have a wide variety of flavors! For a list of our full menu, click the menu button on the top of the page.

Is there a risk of allergens and cross contamination?

Yes, there is a risk of cross contamition with allergens including dairy, soy and tree nuts.

Are you open all year round?

Yes! We are open all year round and even have special flavors to celebrate each season and holiday!

Are you a chain company or a local business?

We are a smaller chain company! We have 3 locations but are seeking to grow in the coming years.

Do you have non-dairy options?

Yes, we have lots of options for those who do not want dairy ice cream! To view our full menu, click the menu button on the top of the page.

Do you have sugar free and fat free options?

Yes, we have lots of flavors and options that are sugar free and/or fat free! To view our full menu, click the menu button on the top of the page.

Do you have dine in or takeout options?

Both! You can sit inside in our large seating area, you can sit outside on our shaded patio, or you could take it to go!

Do you deliver?

Unfortunately, no we do not deliver. We hope to with the expansion of our company it will be a service offered in the near future!

Are there quarts of ice cream to take to go?

Yes! We have a wide variety of options to take to go that come in 3 diffferent sizes: 36oz, 60oz, 120oz.

Is your ice cream pasteruized?

Yes, all of our products containing dairy are pasteurized.

What are the portion sizes?

One single scoop is approximately 5oz!
